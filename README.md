# PSOC™ 4 Liquid Level Sensing (LLS) using PSOC™ 4000T and PSOC™ 4100T Plus training manual
This training introduces Liquid Level Sensing (LLS) on the 5th generation Multi-Sense Converter with Low-Power (MSCLP) available on the PSOC™ 4000T and PSOC™ 4100T Plus. It introduces the LLS technology, the development ecosystem, the tuning and calibration procedure, and ends with two hands on labs that cover liquid level calibration and foam rejection calibration.  


## Device family
- [PSOC™ 4](https://www.infineon.com/products/microcontroller/32-bit-psoc-arm-cortex/psoc-4-mcu)

## How to use this training?
1. Download the training [content](#content).
2. Watch the video or review the presentation at your own pace.
3. Follow the step-by-step instructions in the training manual during the hands-on sections.

## Training level
- E2: Intermediate

## Pre-requisites 
- ModusToolbox™ with Eclipse IDE
- Basic understanding of ModusToolbox™ 

See [training content](#content) for versions and instructions.

## Hardware
- [CY8CPROTO-040T-MS](https://www.infineon.com/evaluation-board/CY8CPROTO-040T-MS)

## Duration
- ~90 min video with hands-on labs

## Agenda
1. Introduction to Infineon's Liquid Level Sensing (LLS)
2. Development ecosystem
3. Tuning and calibration
4. Lab 1: Liquid Level Sensing (LLS) factory calibration
5. Lab 2: LLS foam rejection calibration

## Expected outcome
- Become familiar with Liquid Level Sensing (LLS) on the 5th generation MSCLP block
- Learn about the development ecosystem and how to calibrate a LLS system
- Become proficient with the LLS calibration and foam rejection calibration procedures
